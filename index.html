    About

    Lilac and Lavender is a family-owned and operated lavender farm located in the heart of the countryside. We love hosting local events, selling fresh flowers and fragrances, and providing tours of our picturesque fields.

    Our farm is a peaceful and serene oasis, filled with the sweet scent of lavender. We grow several varieties of lavender, which bloom throughout the year. Our fields are carefully tended by our team of experienced farmers, who are dedicated to producing the highest quality lavender products possible.

    We host a variety of events throughout the year, including festivals, workshops, and educational tours. Our events are designed to be fun and engaging for visitors of all ages. We believe that everyone can benefit from spending time in nature and learning about the natural world around them.

    In addition to our events, we also sell a range of lavender products, including fresh flowers, fragrances, and essential oils. Our products are all made from the highest quality lavender grown right here on our farm. We take great care to ensure that our products are both beautiful and effective, and we are confident that you will love them as much as we do.

    If you're interested in learning more about lavender or just want to spend some time in nature, we invite you to join us for a tour of our farm. Our tours are led by knowledgeable guides who can answer any questions you may have about lavender or our farm. You'll get to see our fields up close and learn about the process of growing and harvesting lavender.
